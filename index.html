App Guide

Welcome to Trackly! This guide will help you navigate and use the key features of the app.

1. Task management

Trackly lets you manage your tasks dynamically. Here's how you can interact with the app:

  • Click to add an extra task block. You can add up to 7 extra tasks.
  • Click to mark a task as completed.
  • Click to clear/delete a task

2. Extra Features

Trackly offers a few extra features to enhance your experience. Click to access:

  • Clears all tasks and progress, resetting the app to its initial state.
  • Opens a pomodoro timer that helps improve your focus & productivity. You can add this and the app in a tab group.
  • Allows you to choose from Spotify, Apple Music, or YouTube Music to play background sounds while working. Sign in to your account to change playlist.
  • The top progress bar visually updates as you complete tasks. Hover on it to see your progress percentage.

3. App Behavior

  • Data Persistence: Your tasks and progress are restored even after refreshing or closing the tab. Make sure to use the same browser on the next session.
  • Auto Dark Mode: The app follows your device's theme and automatically enables dark mode.
  • Installable App: You can install Trackly on your mobile or PC by clicking the "Install App" button in Chrome.
